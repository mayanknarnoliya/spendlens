# ECONOMICS.md — Unit Economics

## What a Converted Lead Is Worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits. Assume:
- Average deal: $5,000 in credits at 30% discount → Credex margin ~$1,500 per deal
- Repeat purchase rate: 40% within 6 months (companies re-buy as credits are consumed)
- LTV (18-month estimate): $1,500 × 1.4 repeat factor = ~$2,100 per converted customer

**Estimated LTV per converted customer: $2,100**

This is conservative. Companies spending $500+/mo on AI tools are spending $6,000+/year — the credits market for them is significant.

**Blended CAC assumption (organic phase): ~$0–$50 per consultation booked**

At $2,100 LTV and ~$50 CAC, payback period is essentially immediate for organic channels.

## Conversion Funnel

```
Landing page visitors: 1,000
↓ 35% start the audit form
350 form starts
↓ 80% complete and submit
280 audits completed
↓ 30% of completions capture email
84 emails captured
↓ Of those, 25% are "high savings" (>$500/mo)
21 high-savings leads
↓ 15% book a Credex consultation (from high-savings segment)
~3 consultations booked per 1,000 visitors
↓ 50% of consultations convert to credit purchase
~1.5 credit purchases per 1,000 visitors
```

**Revenue per 1,000 visitors: ~$3,150** (1.5 deals × $2,100 LTV)

## What Would Have to Be True for $1M ARR in 18 Months

$1M ARR = ~$55,500/month in Credex revenue from SpendLens-sourced deals.

At $2,100 LTV and 18-month amortization: need ~476 closed deals from SpendLens in 18 months = ~26 deals/month.

At 50% consultation → close rate: need 53 consultations/month.
At 15% lead → consultation rate (high-savings segment): need 353 high-savings leads/month.
At 25% of emails being high-savings: need 1,412 emails/month.
At 30% audit → email capture: need 4,707 audits/month.

**Is 4,707 audits/month achievable in 18 months?**

Month 1: ~300 audits (HN launch + organic)
Month 6: ~1,500 audits (growing SEO, newsletter features, word-of-mouth)
Month 12: ~3,500 audits (embedded widget live, partner channels active)
Month 18: ~6,000 audits (compound word-of-mouth, SEO matured)

**Verdict:** Achievable, but requires the partner co-marketing channel to activate by month 6. The organic-only path gets to ~$300K ARR; partner channels push it to $1M+. The key assumption is that Credex's existing vendor relationships can drive warm leads at volume — that's the unfair advantage.
