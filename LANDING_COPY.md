## FAQ — 5 Real Questions

**Q: Is this actually free?**
Yes. No trial, no credit card, no plan. SpendLens is a free tool built by Credex. The business model is that some users will want to act on the savings via Credex's discounted credits — but that's always optional and never required to get full audit results.

**Q: Do you store my spend data?**
Your tool and spend data stays in your browser (localStorage) unless you choose to submit your email. If you submit your email, we store the audit result (tool names, savings amounts) but never sell it or share it with third parties.

**Q: How do you know the pricing is accurate?**
Every number in the audit engine traces back to an official vendor pricing page with a verified date. We re-verify pricing weekly. The sources are public at PRICING_DATA.md in our GitHub repo.

**Q: My company uses a negotiated enterprise price — will this work?**
The audit uses published list prices as benchmarks. If your negotiated rate is already below our benchmark, you'll see "spending well" for that tool. If you're paying above list (common with older enterprise contracts), we'll flag it.

**Q: What's Credex?**
Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits — Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise, and others — sourced from companies that overforecast usage or pivoted. The discount is real. SpendLens is a free tool that surfaces savings opportunities; for high-savings cases, Credex is one option for capturing more of those savings. There's no pressure to use Credex — the audit is useful regardless.
