## Notes on Pricing Accuracy

- Enterprise pricing is always "starting from" — actual contracts vary. We use published floor prices or best available estimates clearly noted.
- API-direct tiers are excluded from per-seat recommendations since they're usage-based. Users on API plans should do a separate usage analysis.
- Prices shown are in USD. International pricing may vary.
- We re-verify all prices weekly before any deployment update. Stale prices will be flagged in a future version.
